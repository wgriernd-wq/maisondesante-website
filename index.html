The pivot from financial integrity to biological vitality.

My journey to the desert began in the boardroom. Before I was Dr. Grier, I was a corporate auditor — spending years identifying risks and finding hidden insolvencies in complex financial systems. But I realized that while I was balancing ledgers, the people around me were physically running on empty.

I sold my home, left the corporate world, and moved to Chicago to pursue a dual Doctorate in Naturopathic Medicine and a Master's in Acupuncture. I didn't leave my auditor's mind behind — I simply applied it to a more complex system: the human body.

Born and raised in Louisiana, I know that health isn't just about restriction — it's about balance. The Magnolia is our state flower for a reason: elegant, rooted, and bold.
